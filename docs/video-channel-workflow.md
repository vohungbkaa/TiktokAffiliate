# Video Va Channel Workflow

## Kenh TikTok

Goi y positioning dau tien:

```text
Dev test snack deadline
```

Ly do:

- Co nhan vat va boi canh ro.
- Phu hop voi nguoi dung la dan van phong, sinh vien, developer.
- Mo rong duoc sang snack, cafe, ban phim, setup lam viec, productivity gear.

## Channel Setup

Thong tin kenh:

```text
Ten kenh: Dev An Dem
Bio: Test snack, do an vat va combo cuu doi luc deadline.
Avatar: mat that hoac logo don gian.
Tone: that, ngan, co du lieu, khong noi qua.
```

Content pillars:

- Snack code dem.
- Do an van phong.
- Combo duoi 100k.
- Test mon viral.
- Mon dang mua/khong dang mua.
- Doc review xau roi kiem chung.

## Video Formats

### POV

```text
"POV: 11 gio dem, bug chua fix va ban mo goi snack nay."
```

### Ranking

```text
"Xep hang 5 mon snack cay dang mua tren TikTok Shop."
```

### Bad Review Check

```text
"Doc review 1 sao cua mon nay xong toi muon test thu xem co dung khong."
```

### Office Snack

```text
"Mon nay an o van phong co on khong: co ban tay, mui co nong, co de do khong?"
```

### Late Night

```text
"Neu hay lam viec khuya, day la combo duoi 100k nen test."
```

## Video Structure

Video 15-25 giay:

```text
0-2s: Hook
2-6s: Show product/problem
6-15s: Detail/reason/review/social proof
15-22s: Verdict
22-25s: CTA
```

CTA mau:

```text
"Minh de san pham o gio hang, nho doc review va check voucher truoc khi mua."
```

## Su Dung AI

Dung AI de:

- Viet hook.
- Viet script.
- Tao caption.
- Tao voice-over.
- Tao subtitle.
- Tao bien the format.

Khong dung AI de:

- Gia vo da an neu chua an.
- Gia mao nguoi noi tieng/creator khac.
- Tao endorsement gia.
- Reup video cua nguoi khac.

Neu video la AI-generated realistic hoac bi chinh sua dang ke bang AI, nen bat label AI theo chinh sach TikTok.

## Workflow Tao Video

### Ban Dau

```text
Product Score cao
  -> AI sinh 5 ideas
  -> Chon 1 idea
  -> Tao voice/subtitle
  -> Edit CapCut
  -> Dang TikTok
  -> Nhap metric
```

### Khi Co San Pham Thang

```text
Winning product
  -> Mua mau
  -> Quay footage that
  -> Tao 10 bien the hook
  -> Dang theo lich
  -> Do view/click/order
```

## Posting Plan 30 Ngay

```text
Ngay 1-7:
2 video/ngay, tap trung test format va hook.

Ngay 8-21:
2-3 video/ngay, moi san pham 3 angle.

Ngay 22-30:
Scale san pham co don, lam ranking va comparison.
```

## Tracking Sau Khi Dang

Moi video can nhap:

- Views.
- Likes.
- Comments.
- Shares.
- Product clicks.
- Orders.
- Commission.

Quy tac:

```text
View cao, click thap:
Video giai tri duoc nhung offer/CTA yeu.

Click cao, order thap:
San pham/shop/page yeu.

View thap, order tot:
San pham tot, can hook moi.

View cao, click cao, order cao:
Nhan ban format.
```

## Ads Workflow

Chi chay ads khi:

- Video co don organic.
- View to click >= 1%.
- Click to order >= 3%.
- Hoa hong moi don du de cover CPA.

Test:

```text
Budget: 100k-300k/ngay/video
Thoi gian: 2-3 ngay
Dung neu CPA > hoa hong/đon
```
