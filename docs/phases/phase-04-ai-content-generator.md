# Phase 04: AI Content Generator

## Muc Tieu

Bien san pham da duoc chon thanh y tuong video co the san xuat nhanh.

## Tasks

- Tao model `ContentIdea`.
- Tao generator cho hook.
- Tao generator cho script 15s/30s.
- Tao generator cho caption.
- Tao generator cho hashtags.
- Tao shot list.
- Tao CTA.
- Tao claim warning.
- Cham `content_potential_score` neu phase 3 chua co du tin hieu.
- Sinh `visual_hook`, `pain_point`, `demo_idea`, `before_after_angle` cho tung product.
- Lien ket content idea voi ly do chon san pham trong `ProductScore`.
- Tao status flow: `idea -> selected -> produced -> posted -> measured`.

## Video Formats

```text
POV
Ranking
Review
Comparison
Bad review check
Office snack
Late night
Combo
```

## Definition Of Done

- Chon 1 product co the sinh 10 ideas.
- Moi idea co hook, script, caption, hashtag, CTA.
- Co shot list de quay/edit.
- Co it nhat 3 angle gan voi pain point/review/benefit that cua san pham.
- Co canh bao claim khong nen noi qua.
- Co the mark idea thanh selected/produced.

## Output

```text
ContentIdea records
Hook variants
Script variants
Caption/hashtag/CTA
Shot list
Claim warnings
Content potential signals
```
