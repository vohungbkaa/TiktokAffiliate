# Phase 08: Ads Scaling

## Muc Tieu

Chi chay ads cho video/san pham da co tin hieu organic, tranh dot tien vao san pham chua duoc validate.

## Dieu Kien Test Ads

```text
View -> Click >= 1%
Click -> Order >= 3%
Co don organic
Commission per 1,000 views du tot
Shop va review on dinh
```

## Tasks

- Tao model `AdCampaign`.
- Nhap spend.
- Nhap orders/commission tu ads.
- Tinh CPA.
- Tinh profit proxy.
- Tao rule stop/scale.
- Tao ads performance dashboard.

## Test Plan

```text
Budget: 100k-300k/ngay/video
Thoi gian: 2-3 ngay
Dung neu CPA > hoa hong/đon
Scale neu CPA thap hon hoa hong va refund/cancel on
```

## Definition Of Done

- Biet video nao du dieu kien test ads.
- Biet ads dang loi hay lo.
- Co rule dung campaign khi CPA qua cao.
- Co lich su ads theo video/product.

## Luu Y

Voi do an vat, ads co the kho loi vi hoa hong moi don thap. Ads chi nen dung de scale video da win, khong dung de cuu video/san pham yeu.
